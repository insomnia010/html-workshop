I was 7 when Mom got two Seal point Siamese kittens, Phu Cat (foo) and Bein (ben) Cat. Bein died of Feline Leukemia in 1976, Phu Cat lived to be 19. He was my kitty, no doubt, from first grade to high school graduation, (he was in my Senior picture) to my enlistment in the Coast Guard, and my return to Las Vegas in 1983. He was cremated, and Mom and I buried his ashes under his favorite rose bush, a beautiful yellow called Honor.
